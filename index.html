About Us

Welcome to SoundFidelity. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of Hi-Fi headphones, with an emphasis on quality and customer support.

Founded in 2021 by Amanda Walser and Giancarlo Metitieri, SoundFidelity has come a long way from its beginnings in Zurich, Switzerland. When they first started out, their passion for high-end headphones drove them to start their own business.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Services

We only sell high-quality headphones. Unlike other stores, the headphones you buy are manually checked by an audio engineer before being sent. All headphones you buy on this website come in a hand-made custom-finished wooden box, made from the Spanish Cedar tree.

Inside, you will find your headphones enclosed in high-end foam material. All your cables and documentation are placed inside a small leather slip case that can also be found inside the box.

Because we only want the best for our customers, you will get a customized Sonarworks SoundID headphone profile for free.
